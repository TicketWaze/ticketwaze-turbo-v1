1. Platform Overview

TicketWaze is a full-stack activity ticketing and management platform targeting the Haitian and Caribbean market with ambitions for broader expansion. It brings together three types of users under one roof:

  • Attendees – people who discover activities, purchase tickets, and attend.
  • Organisers – individuals or teams who create, publish, and manage activities.
  • Admins – platform administrators who monitor the entire system.

The platform is built as a Turborepo monorepo with four Next.js applications:

  • Attendee App – public-facing ticket marketplace.
  • Organisation App – private dashboard for activity organisers.
  • Website App – marketing and informational site.
  • Payment App – MonCash mobile money integration landing page.

The platform supports two languages (French as default, English as secondary), two currencies (HTG and USD), and two payment methods (Stripe for card payments and MonCash for mobile money).

2. Attendee App

The attendee app is the public-facing product. Any visitor can browse activities; creating an account unlocks purchasing, favorites, following, and order history.

2.1 Authentication & Onboarding

Sign Up

  • Register with email and password.
  • Optionally provide a referral code from an existing user.
  • Account verification email is sent after registration.
  • Google OAuth sign-up (one-click, no password needed).

Sign In

  • Sign in with email and password.
  • Google OAuth sign-in.
  • Forgot password → receive reset email → set new password flow.

Onboarding (first-time users)

  • Multi-step profile setup after first login.
  • Option to also create an organisation (for people who are both attendee and organiser).
  • Invited user onboarding (accepting a team invitation from an organisation).

2.2 Activity Discovery & Browsing

Explore Page

  • Grid of all publicly listed activities with activity card previews (image, name, date, price).
  • Real-time search by activity name.
  • Filter activities by category (Conference, Concert, Festival, Comedy, Theater, Tournament, Workshop, Seminar, Networking, Startup, Food Festival, Art, Cultural, Charity, Fundraiser, Meetup, Community, Meet/Virtual).

Activity Detail Page

  • Full activity name, cover image, and description.
  • Organiser name with verified organisation badge and profile link.
  • All activity dates and times, including timezone.
  • Physical address displayed, with a Google Maps embed and a direct "Open in Maps" link (in-person activities).
  • Meeting link access for virtual/Meet activities.
  • Performer/artist profiles – clickable, with individual bios.
  • Ticket types and pricing listed.
  • Add to Calendar button (generates calendar activity).
  • Share activity on social media.
  • Add/remove from favorites.
Private activities are invitation-only. Attendees without a valid invitation code cannot view or purchase tickets.

2.3 Checkout & Payment

The checkout flow is a multi-step wizard:

Step 1 – Ticket Selection

  • Choose ticket type and quantity.
  • Real-time pricing summary updates.
  • Free activities skip the payment step entirely.

Step 2 – Recipient Information

  • Enter attendee details (for self, or on behalf of someone else).
  • Registered users have their info pre-filled.
  • Guest checkout is supported (no account required for free activities or some paid flows).

Step 3 – Payment

  • Choose Stripe (international credit/debit card) or MonCash (Haitian mobile money).
  • Full fee breakdown shown (ticket price + service fee).
  • Stripe uses an embedded checkout component.
  • MonCash redirects to the dedicated payment app for confirmation.

Step 4 – Order Summary & Confirmation

  • Review complete order before finalising.
  • Confirmation email sent automatically after successful payment.
  • Order success page shown, with a link to view tickets.

2.4 Ticket Management & Upcoming Activities

Upcoming Activities List

  • Shows all activities the attendee has tickets for.
  • Each card links to the full activity page with ticket viewer.

Activity Page (Post-Purchase)

  • View purchased ticket(s) with QR code.
  • Download ticket as PDF or image.
  • View full activity details and location.
  • Rate/evaluate an activity after it has taken place.

Return / Refund

  • Free ticket return – attendee can return a free ticket if the organiser has enabled returns.
  • Paid ticket refund – attendee can request a refund if the organiser has enabled refunds and within the allowed window based on activity dates.
  • Service fees are non-refundable.

Activity History

  • Past activities are listed separately.
  • Post-activity evaluation/rating can be submitted from here.

2.5 Favorites

  • Heart/bookmark any activity from its detail page or the explore feed.
  • Dedicated Liked Activities page showing all saved activities.
  • Remove activities from favorites at any time.
  • Favorites persist across sessions.

2.6 Organiser Profiles & Following

Organiser Directory

  • Browse all organisations on the platform.
  • Follow or unfollow any organisation.
  • View the list of organisations you follow.

Organisation Profile Page

  • Organisation name, profile image, description, and verified badge.
  • Follower count.
  • List of upcoming activities by this organiser.
  • List of past activities by this organiser.
  • Contact details (email, website).

2.7 User Profile & Preferences

Profile Page

  • View personal information and statistics.
  • Update name, avatar, and contact details.

Preferences

  • Email notifications – toggle which emails you receive.
  • Interest categories – set preferred activity types for personalised recommendations.
  • Language – switch between French and English.
  • Currency – choose between HTG and USD display.
  • Privacy settings.

2.8 Wallet & Transactions

  • View wallet balance in both HTG and USD.
  • Full transaction history with pagination (10 items per page).
  • Each transaction shows type, amount, date, and status.
  • History of all orders and payments made.

2.9 Deletion Status Notices

When an organiser initiates deletion of an activity, attendees who have tickets are proactively informed via a prominent banner on the activity's upcoming page:

  • Pending Deletion banner (amber) – displayed when deletionStatus = "pending_deletion". Informs the attendee that the organiser has requested deletion but may still cancel it. States that tickets will be refunded if the activity is deleted, and that service fees are non-refundable.
  • Deleted banner (red) – displayed when deletionStatus = "deleted". Informs the attendee the activity no longer exists and a ticket refund has been initiated. Confirms service fees are non-refundable.

3. Organisation App

The organisation app is the private dashboard for activity organisers. Access is gated by authentication and role-based permissions. Each organisation can have multiple members with different permission levels.

3.1 Authentication & Setup

  • Login with email and password.
  • Forgot password flow.
  • Password reset.
  • Accept team invitation (invited members set their password and complete onboarding).
  • Multi-step organisation setup wizard during first login.
  • Session maintains an active organisation context (users who manage multiple organisations can switch).

3.2 Activity Creation

Two activity types are supported:

In-Person Activity

A 3-step wizard:

  • Step 1 – Basic Details
    • Activity name, description, and category (17 categories available).
    • Cover image upload with in-browser crop/resize tool.
    • Physical address, city, state, country, and geographic coordinates (map picker).
    • Activity tags for discoverability.
    • Performers / artists management (add multiple with names and bios).
    • Visibility: public or private (invitation-only).
  • Step 2 – Dates & Times
    • Add one or multiple activity days.
    • Each day has its own start time, end time, and timezone.
    • Membership tier limits the number of days allowed.
  • Step 3 – Ticket Classes
    • Free or paid activity toggle.
    • Add multiple ticket types with name, description, price, and quantity.
    • Refundable toggle per ticket class.
    • Currency selection (HTG or USD).

Meet / Virtual Activity

Same 3-step wizard, but:

  • No physical location — Google Meet link is generated automatically via Google OAuth.
  • Address and map fields are replaced with virtual activity details.
Activity creation validates all basic details server-side before advancing to the next step. A "no changes" guard prevents re-submitting identical data.

3.3 Activity Editing

Organisers can edit any activity that has not started and is not pending deletion. The same 3-step wizard is reused, pre-filled with existing values.

  • Edit activity name, description, or cover image.
  • Edit dates and times.
  • Edit ticket types, prices, and quantities.
  • Edit location details.
  • Edit performers.
  • Edit tags.

Post-Update Alerts

After a successful save, the organiser sees an inline alert explaining what happens next:

  • Warning (amber) – if the organiser changed the activity name, description, or cover image: the activity is re-submitted for review and temporarily unlisted (1–2 business days).
  • Success (green) – if only dates, location, tickets, or tags changed: the update goes live immediately with no re-review required.

Deletion Guard

  • If an activity's deletion has been requested, the edit page redirects the organiser back to the activity detail page — editing is blocked server-side.
  • In the activity drawer UI, the edit button is replaced with a non-clickable amber warning: "This activity is pending deletion and cannot be edited."

3.4 Activity Deletion

  • Organiser can request deletion of any of their activities.
  • Deletion status progresses: null → pending_deletion → deleted.
  • Organiser can cancel the deletion request while it is still in pending_deletion state.
  • Once fully deleted, the platform initiates automatic refunds to all ticket holders (ticket price only; service fees are non-refundable).

3.5 Activity Viewing & Detail

Activities List

  • Grid of all activities created by the organisation.
  • Quick stats per activity card: ticket count, revenue, status.

Activity Detail Drawer

  • Slide-over drawer with full activity information.
  • Activity dates and times.
  • Location address.
  • Edit button (if activity is upcoming and not pending deletion).
  • Pending-deletion warning replaces the edit button when applicable.

3.6 Attendee Management & Check-In

  • View full attendee list for any activity.
  • Search and filter attendees.
  • See each attendee's name, ticket type, and check-in status.
  • Check-in attendees on arrival (manual or QR-scan based).
  • Export attendee data.

3.7 Discount & Promo Codes

  • View all existing discount codes for the organisation.
  • Create a new discount code with:
    • Code string (custom or auto-generated).
    • Discount type: percentage or fixed amount.
    • Usage limit (max number of uses).
    • Expiry date.
  • Track how many times each code has been used.
  • Activate or deactivate codes.

3.8 Team & Role Management

Team Members

  • View all current team members with their roles.
  • Invite new members by email (invitation email sent automatically).
  • Remove existing members.
  • View pending invitations (waitlist).
  • Membership tier determines the maximum number of team members allowed.

Roles & Permissions

Two broad roles exist, with fine-grained permissions:

  • Owner / Admin – full access to all features.
  • Staff – restricted access; specific permissions can be granted:
    • View activities
    • Create activities
    • Edit activities
    • View attendees
    • Check in attendees
    • View discount codes
    • View finance
    • Create withdrawal requests
    • Manage subscriptions
    • View analytics
    • Manage team
    • Update organisation profile

3.9 Organisation Profile & Settings

  • Update organisation name.
  • Upload or replace organisation logo/profile image.
  • Edit organisation bio / description.
  • Update contact email.
  • Update phone number.
  • Update website URL.
  • Set preferred currency (HTG or USD).
  • View current membership tier and its feature limits.

3.10 Finance & Payouts

Finance Dashboard

  • Total revenue earned (all time).
  • Available balance (ready to withdraw).
  • Pending balance (awaiting clearance).
  • Revenue broken down by time period.
  • Full transaction history with date-range filter and pagination.

Withdrawal / Payout

  • Initiate a withdrawal request for available funds.
  • Enter withdrawal amount.
  • Provide bank account or mobile money payout details.
  • Track withdrawal request status (pending, approved, completed).
  • View full withdrawal history.
  • Multiple currencies handled separately.

3.11 Payment Method Configuration

  • Add and configure Stripe account credentials for card payments.
  • Add and configure MonCash account credentials for mobile payments.
  • Set a security PIN for payment operations.
  • View and update existing payment provider details.
  • Both payment methods can be active simultaneously.

3.12 Analytics Dashboard

A comprehensive analytics page available to all tiers, with some advanced metrics restricted to Pro PRO:

Revenue Metrics

  • Total revenue (all time and by time period).
  • Average order value.
  • Monthly revenue trend chart.

Ticket Metrics

  • Total tickets sold.
  • Daily ticket sales chart.
  • Ticket class breakdown (how many of each type sold).
  • Monthly ticket sales trend.

Activity Metrics

  • Total activities created (upcoming and past).
  • Top activities by tickets sold.
  • Top activities by views.
  • Activity views and social shares tracked.
  • Conversion rate (views → purchases).
  • Check-in rate.
  • Refund rate.

Audience Metrics

  • Total followers and follower growth.
  • Guest vs. registered attendee split.
  • Payment provider breakdown (Stripe vs. MonCash usage).
  • Gender distribution PRO – male / female / other breakdown.
  • Audience demographics PRO – detailed demographic data.

Ratings & Reviews

  • Star rating average and distribution PRO.
  • Review count and trends.
Free-tier users see teaser metrics for Pro features with a clear upsell prompt to upgrade.

3.13 Subscriptions & Membership Tiers

  • View current active subscription / tier.
  • Compare all available plans with feature tables.
  • Upgrade to a Pro tier with an in-app payment flow.
  • Upgrade confirmation page after successful payment.
  • Tier limits govern: number of team members, activity days, analytics depth, and invite limits.

3.14 Activity Sharing & Poster Generation

  • Share activity link to social media platforms directly from the dashboard.
  • Generate a visual activity poster (EventPosterGenerator) with activity details pre-filled.
  • Customise and download the poster for offline or social promotion.

3.15 Role-Based Access Control Summary

Every sensitive page and action in the organisation app is gated by a server-side policy check. If a user lacks the required permission, they see an "Unauthorised" view instead of the content. This prevents lower-privilege staff from accidentally or intentionally accessing restricted data.

5. Payment App (MonCash Integration)

A lightweight Next.js app dedicated to the MonCash mobile money payment flow. When an attendee selects MonCash at checkout, they are redirected here.

  • Displays the MonCash payment instructions and confirmation page.
  • Handles the return callback from the MonCash gateway.
  • Communicates payment success or failure back to the attendee app via webhook.
  • Lightweight — no auth required; session is maintained via payment session tokens.

6. Admin Panel

The admin panel gives the TicketWaze internal team visibility and control over the entire platform. It is accessible only to verified admin users.

Authentication

  • Admin login page.
  • Admin registration (invitation-based).
  • Password reset.
  • Invitation acceptance with new password setup.

Dashboard & Monitoring

  • System-wide analytics overview.
  • Attendees management – view and manage all platform users.
  • Organisations management – view, verify, or suspend organisations.
  • Payments monitoring – track all transactions across the platform.
  • Payout / withdrawal request queue – approve or reject organiser withdrawals.
  • Tickets management – view all issued tickets.
  • Activities log – full audit trail of user and system actions.
  • Admin user management – invite and manage other admins.

7. Cross-Platform Features

Internationalisation (i18n)

  • All UI text translated into French and English.
  • Locale-specific date, time, and number formatting.
  • Language switcher available in user preferences.
  • Hreflang tags for SEO on the marketing site.

Notifications

  • Transactional emails for registration, payment confirmation, activity updates, invitations, and password resets.
  • User-configurable notification preferences (opt in / opt out per type).

Mobile Responsiveness

  • Mobile-first design on all apps.
  • Responsive grid layouts that adapt from 1 to 2+ columns.
  • Dedicated mobile navigation (hamburger menus, floating action buttons).
  • Touch-friendly tap targets throughout.

Image Handling

  • Cover image upload with in-browser crop and resize (react-easy-crop).
  • Next.js optimised image delivery (lazy load, responsive sizes).
  • Avatar / profile image upload for users and organisations.

UI / UX Patterns

  • Toast notifications (sonner) for success, error, warning, and info messages.
  • Inline alert banners for important persistent notices.
  • Dialog modals for destructive action confirmations.
  • Animated page transitions (Framer Motion) on multi-step forms.
  • Loading skeleton states while data is fetching.
  • Empty state illustrations with helpful prompts.
  • Form validation with real-time error messages (React Hook Form + Zod).

Search & Filtering

  • Real-time search by activity name on the explore page.
  • Category filter on the explore page.
  • Attendee search and filter in the organiser dashboard.
  • Date-range filter on transaction history.

Pagination

  • Transaction history, attendee lists, and activity lists are paginated server-side.
  • Default page sizes: 10 or 15 items per page.

Google Integrations

  • Google OAuth – sign-in for attendees; Meet activity creation for organisers.
  • Google Maps – embed and direction link for in-person activities.
  • Google Meet – auto-generated meeting links for virtual activities.

Security

  • JWT-based authentication with bearer tokens.
  • Sessions managed via NextAuth.
  • Server-side role and permission checks on every protected page and action.
  • Payment PIN for sensitive financial operations.

Data Export

  • Attendee data export from the organiser dashboard.
  • Ticket download (PDF/image) for attendees.
  • Activity poster download for organisers.
